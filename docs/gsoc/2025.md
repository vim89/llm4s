# GSoC 2025
{: .fs-9 }

LLM4S was selected for GSoC 2025 under the Scala Center Organization.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

This year, we are focused on "Engineering GenAI Agents in Functional Scala." Our selected contributors are working on critical features ranging from Agentic Toolkits to RAG pipelines and Observability.
